Experiencing a moderation action in a popular open-world roleplay game can be frustrating. To regain access to your account, you must navigate the official **Emergency Response Liberty County ban appeal** system. Moderation actions are handled directly by the Police Roleplay Community (PRC) staff team. 

Before submitting an appeal, you must identify the type of ban active on your account. The moderation system distinguishes between temporary bans, permanent game bans, and private server bans.

| Ban Type | Duration | Appealable? | Handling Authority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Temporary Ban** | 1 to 30 days | Yes | PRC Moderation Team |

To file an official **Emergency Response Liberty County ban appeal**, you must use the designated community platforms. PRC utilizes Discord and external support desks to process moderation inquiries systematically. Follow these structured steps to prepare and submit your case.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Join the Official Discord Server">
    Navigate to the official [Police Roleplay Community Discord](https://discord.gg/prc) server. If your Discord account is also banned or restricted, you must use the web-based support portal provided on their official website at [erlc.gg](https://erlc.gg/).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Support Ticket System">
    Once inside the PRC Discord, locate the support channel or utility bot. Open a new moderation ticket. Do not send direct messages to individual staff members or developers, as this violates community rules and will delay your request.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Appeal Template">
    Fill out the appeal form with precise details. You must provide your exact Roblox username, your Discord ID, the date of the moderation action, and the specific reason displayed on your ban screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Provide Honest Context and Evidence">
    Explain the circumstances surrounding your ban clearly. If you made a mistake, acknowledge the infraction and outline how you will follow the rules in the future. If you believe the ban was a mistake, attach clear video or screenshot evidence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Submission Tip">
