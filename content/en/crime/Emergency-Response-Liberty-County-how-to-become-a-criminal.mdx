### Bank Robbery Strategy
To successfully hit the River City Bank, one crew member must focus on drilling the vault while another manages security cameras and guards the lobby. Police response times are incredibly fast during bank alarms, so setting up a block vehicle outside the entrance to absorb initial gunfire is highly recommended.

### Jewelry Store Run
The Jewelry Store relies on speed. Use your glass cutter quietly, grab the high-value diamonds, and exit through the rear door before the police can block the front entrance. Avoid using major highways during your escape, as officers will set up spike strips.

<Callout type="warning" title="Spike Strip Warning">
If your tires are popped by police spike strips, your vehicle's speed drops by 80%. Always look ahead at the road surface when driving through major intersections during a pursuit.
