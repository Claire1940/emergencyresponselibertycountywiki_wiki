<Section icon={Target} title="Exterior and Interior Features">

The chop shop is designed to blend into the industrial outskirts of Liberty County, yet it contains distinct visual markers that make it easily recognizable to experienced criminals. Understanding these details helps you identify the building quickly during high-speed police pursuits.

### Exterior Layout
The outside of the building features a run-down, industrial aesthetic. Rusted car parts, discarded exhaust pipes, and various unassorted scrap metal pieces litter the yard. The most notable landmark is a scrapped, heavily rusted Falcon Prime Eques 2003 sitting right outside the entrance. This vehicle serves as an excellent visual cue when driving down Vine Street at high speeds.

### Interior Layout
Inside, the workshop is filled with automotive clutter, including loose car hoods, engines, crates, barrels, and a non-functional vehicle lift. The two most important elements inside are Eddie Moreno and the interactive vehicle board. The board displays real-time information regarding the most valuable vehicles currently wanted on the streets.

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Steal and Sell Vehicles">

Selling a stolen vehicle requires speed, navigation skills, and a solid understanding of the delivery mechanics. You cannot simply drive any car to Eddie; you must follow a structured process to ensure the transaction goes smoothly without law enforcement interfering.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Target Vehicle">
    Check the vehicle board inside the chop shop to see which car class or model is currently in high demand. If you cannot find the exact vehicle, look for high-tier civilian cars parked around town.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire the Vehicle">
    Approach the target car and use your criminal tools to break in and hotwire the ignition. Be prepared for the vehicle's alarm to trigger, which may alert nearby police officers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate to Postal Code 501">
    Drive the stolen vehicle toward Vine Street. Avoid main highways if possible, utilizing backroads and dirt paths to keep a low profile and minimize the chances of running into police roadblocks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deliver to Eddie Moreno">
    Pull the vehicle directly into the designated drop-off area at the chop shop. Step inside, interact with Eddie Moreno, and confirm the sale to receive your cash reward instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Clean Deliveries Pay More">
The physical condition of the vehicle affects your final payout. Avoid crashing into guardrails, trees, or police cruisers during your escape to ensure you receive the maximum cash value from Eddie.
</Callout>

</Section>
