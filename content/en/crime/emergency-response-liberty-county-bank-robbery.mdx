<Section icon={Target} title="The Vault Progression Phase">

Once you have breached the perimeter, the clock begins ticking. The **emergency response liberty county bank robbery** is not a simple "point and shoot" interaction; it involves a multi-stage process that requires navigation of the bank's restricted staff areas and the subterranean vault level.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Passcode">
    Search the staff area and offices for a hidden **four-digit passcode**. This code is randomized for every heist and is required to open the entrance gate leading to the basement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Breach the Gate">
    Input the discovered code into the keypad at the entrance gate. Once the gate opens, move quickly to the underground level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Plant Explosives">
    Approach the massive vault door and hold the interaction button to plant charges. **Move back immediately**; the explosion deals lethal damage to anyone standing too close.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Loot the Vault">
    Enter the vault to collect stacks of cash and gold slabs. You must be fast, as the vault's internal defense systems will activate shortly after the breach.
  </Step>
</Steps>

During the looting phase, players must balance greed with safety. The vault contains both cash (faster to collect) and gold (higher value but heavier). As the robbery progresses, the bank's automated systems will attempt to neutralize the intruders.

| Hazard | Trigger Time | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Security Lasers** | Immediately after blast | High damage on contact |
| **Tear Gas** | When vault timer expires | Continuous health drain |
| **LEO Response** | 0-60 seconds post-alarm | Arrest and heist failure |

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Tip">
Designate one player to watch the stairs for incoming police while the others focus on emptying the vault. Communication is key to surviving the tear gas deployment.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Escape Routes and the 10-Minute Rule">

The most difficult part of the **emergency response liberty county bank robbery** isn't getting the money—it's keeping it. The Bank of River City features a unique escape tunnel that allows criminals to bypass the front lobby, which is usually swarming with police by the time the vault is empty.

To use the tunnel, you must locate a secondary code found beneath one of the gold slabs inside the vault. Once entered, you can shoot down a vent in the tunnel system that leads directly to the main road tunnel outside the bank. This exit is significantly safer than walking out the front door into a hail of gunfire.

However, the heist is not considered "complete" until you survive the mandatory cooldown period. The 2026 mechanics enforce strict rules on how you must behave after leaving the bank.

| Action | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dying** | Total loss of stolen funds |
| **Leaving Game** | Total loss of stolen funds |
| **Switching Teams** | Total loss of stolen funds |

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
