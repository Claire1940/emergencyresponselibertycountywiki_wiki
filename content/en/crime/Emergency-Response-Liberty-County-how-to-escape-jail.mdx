Understanding the layout and security protocols of the Liberty County Jail is the first step to planning a successful breakout. The jail layout consists of three primary zones: the cell blocks, the common courtyard, and the administrative security offices. Security systems monitor these areas constantly, and certain actions will trigger automatic alarms.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Sewer Escape Route">

The sewer route is the most reliable path for solo players who do not have pre-stashed tools or active accomplices waiting outside. Follow these steps carefully to navigate the underground tunnels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Courtyard">
    Wait for the scheduled recreation period or use a basic lockpick to exit your cell block and enter the open courtyard area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Sewer Grate">
    Search the perimeter of the courtyard near the rear walls to find the active maintenance sewer grate. 
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Bypass the Grate Cover">
    Interact with the grate cover. If you have a tool, the interaction time is significantly reduced. Without tools, you must complete a brief physical interaction puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate the Tunnels">
    Drop into the sewer system. Follow the main pipeline straight toward the river exit. Avoid turning into dead-end drainage pipes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Exit to the Riverbank">
    Climb the exit ladder to emerge along the riverbank outside the main jail security perimeter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Escape Complete">
Once you emerge from the sewer, your wanted level will instantly set to active. Move away from the riverbank immediately, as police units frequently patrol the nearby bridges.
</Callout>
