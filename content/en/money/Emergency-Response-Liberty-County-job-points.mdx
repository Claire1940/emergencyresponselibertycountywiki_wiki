<Section icon={Briefcase} title="Understanding Job Points and Progression">

In Emergency Response: Liberty County (ER:LC), your progression is split into two main currencies: cash and department experience, commonly referred to as **Emergency Response Liberty County job points**. While cash allows you to purchase civilian vehicles and custom cosmetics, job points are required to climb the ranks of the county's emergency departments. 

Each department features a dedicated ranking system. Accumulating job points unlocks better patrol vehicles, specialized gear, and tactical options like SWAT or heavy rescue apparatus. Unlike cash, department ranks cannot be bypassed with Robux purchases, making active gameplay and efficient farming routes essential for progression.

| Department | Starting Rank | Max Rank | Primary XP Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Police RCPD** | Cadet | Commander | Arrests, traffic stops, call responses |
| **Sheriff LCSO** | Recruit | Chief Deputy | Highway patrols, warrants, arrests |
| **Fire & Rescue** | Volunteer | Battalion Chief | Fire suppression, medical treatments |
| **DOT** | Trainee | Senior Technician | Road repairs, vehicle towing, hazards |

<Callout type="tip" title="Focus on Ranks">
Do not spread your playtime too thin. Choose one department and grind its ranks to unlock top-tier vehicles before moving to another team.
