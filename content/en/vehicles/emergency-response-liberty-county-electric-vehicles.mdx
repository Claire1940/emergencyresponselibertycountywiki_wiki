<Section icon={Target} title="How to Charge Your Electric Vehicle">

Maintaining your **emergency response liberty county electric vehicles** requires a different approach than traditional refueling. You must monitor your battery percentage and locate charging stations throughout Liberty County.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Charging Station">
    Open your map and look for the lightning bolt icons. These are usually located at gas stations or dedicated parking lots in the city center.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Charging Speed">
    Drive your vehicle into the designated charging bay. You will be prompted with two options: Fast Charge (costs money) or Slow Charge (free).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor the UI">
    A dedicated Charging UI will appear on your screen showing the current battery percentage and the estimated time to reach 100%.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Disconnect and Drive">
    Once the battery is sufficiently charged, simply drive out of the bay. The cost for Fast Charging will be automatically deducted from your balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
Always use the free Slow Charge while you are AFK or roleplaying inside a building. This eliminates fuel costs entirely over time.
</Callout>
