### Air Support Availability & Cost

While the helicopter is an incredibly powerful asset, it is balanced by strict server cooldowns and rank restrictions. Law enforcement officers must strategically time their requests to maximize the three-minute flight window.

| Feature | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Flight Duration** | Approximately 3 minutes per call |
| **Server Cooldown** | 10 minutes after any helicopter dispatch |
| **Player Cooldown** | 1 hour (Sergeant+) or 2 hours (Under Sergeant) |
| **Rank Requirement** | Rank 4 (Sergeant) to call for free |
| **Bypass Method** | Pay Robux to bypass personal cooldowns |

<Callout type="warning" title="Server Cooldown Warning">
Even if your personal cooldown is active or you are willing to pay Robux to bypass it, you cannot summon the helicopter if the 10-minute server-wide cooldown is still active. Always check the dispatch interface before planning a coordinate strike.
</Callout>

### Law Enforcement vs. Criminal Impact

The presence of a helicopter completely changes the map meta. Below is a breakdown of how the air support unit impacts both sides of the law.

| Law Enforcement Impact | Criminal Impact |
| --- | --- |
| Marks suspects with a blinking red circle | Spotlight exposure reveals real-time location |
| Hovering over the circle reveals the suspect's username | Leaving the server while in spotlight triggers a 5-minute jail penalty |
| Radio broadcasts real-time location and nearest landmarks | Must seek overhead cover (tunnels, buildings) to break line of sight |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Spotlight Strategy">
When a criminal is trapped in the helicopter's spotlight, they are highly vulnerable. If they attempt to combat-log (leave the game to avoid arrest) while illuminated, the game automatically treats it as leaving in handcuffs, placing them in jail for 5 minutes.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Request Helicopter Air Support">

Deploying the air support unit requires accessing the law enforcement dispatch menu. Follow these steps to successfully call in a helicopter search.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Dispatch Menu">
    Open your law enforcement interaction menu and select the **Request Air Support** option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Mission Mode">
    Choose between **Patrol** (map-wide sweep) or **Locate Player** (specific target tracking).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Suspect (If Locating)">
    If you selected Locate Player, browse the list of active wanted players and confirm your target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and Deploy">
    Confirm the dispatch. The dispatch radio will announce: *"Air support called inbound. Searching for suspect."*
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Radio Dispatch Logs">
Keep your ears on the police radio. The AI pilot will broadcast messages such as: *"Suspect is on Postal Area [Number], near [Landmark]"* or *"Suspect in handcuffs. Going on Patrol."* This helps ground units coordinate their intercept routes.
</Callout>
