The financial aspect of selling vehicles in Liberty County requires careful consideration. The game enforces a strict depreciation model that significantly reduces the return on your investment. When you sell a vehicle, you will receive substantially less than what you originally paid.

Typically, the dealership offers between 50% and 60% of the original purchase price, resulting in a net loss of 40% to 50%. For example, a vehicle like the Chrysler Champion, which retail for approximately $50,000 to $60,000, will only net you around $28,000 at the sales desk. This steep reduction means that selling vehicles is highly inefficient for generating wealth.
