<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Tactics and FAQ">

Mastering the **Emergency Response Liberty County best full auto gun for police** requires more than just pointing and clicking. You must understand how to use cover and when to tap-fire versus when to hold the trigger. At long ranges, the G36C and M4A1 both suffer from significant damage drop-off, meaning you should fire in 3-5 round bursts to maintain accuracy.

**Tactical Engagement Rules:**
1. **The High Ground**: Always try to fire from an elevated position or from behind your patrol car's engine block for cover.
2. **Reload Management**: Never wait until your magazine is at 0. Reload during "lulls" in the gunfight when the suspect is behind cover.
3. **Headshot Priority**: In ERLC, headshots bypass a significant portion of the "time-to-kill" (TTK). Practice your flick shots in the training range.
