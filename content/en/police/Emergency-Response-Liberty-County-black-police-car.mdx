<Section icon={Coins} title="How to Unlock and Earn Department XP">

Unlocking the best patrol vehicles and customization options for your police fleet requires earning cash and department XP. You cannot skip rank progression by purchasing cash, so consistent active duty is essential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Join the Law Enforcement Team">
    Select either the Police or Sheriff team from the team selection menu, equip your uniform, and spawn your rank's default patrol vehicle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Monitor the Mobile Data Terminal (MDT)">
    Keep your MDT open to track active emergency calls, reported crimes, and suspect locations across Liberty County.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conduct Traffic Stops">
    Use your Radar Gun to catch speeding civilian vehicles. Pull them over, check their records in the MDT, and issue citations using your Citation Book.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Arrests and Transport Suspects">
    Detain wanted players using handcuffs, secure them in your patrol vehicle's rear seat (or optional rear-seat cage), and transport them to jail to secure massive XP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="XP Efficiency">
Always complete your active calls instead of abandoning them. Working with other officers during major server incidents yields the fastest XP and cash progression.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Wrench} title="Private Server Customization and Livery Rules">

If you manage an ER:LC private server, you can take customization to the next level by uploading custom black police car designs. Using the Custom Livery Pack, server owners can configure unique tactical, ghost, or undercover decals for their community.

All custom designs must strictly follow the official [ER:LC Livery and Ad Guidelines](https://erlc.gg/policies/livery-ad-guidelines) and Roblox moderation rules:

| Rule Category | Allowed Elements | Prohibited Elements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Text & Logos** | High-contrast text, 911 / 999 decals | Web links, QR codes, social handles |
| **Staff Markings** | Server-specific Admin / Mod labels | Generic, misleading staff text |
| **Realism** | Fictional or real-world agency designs | Copyrighted brand logos without permission |
| **Ad Dimensions** | 16:9 Billboards, 2:3 Bus-stops | Giveaways, free Robux promotions |

<Callout type="info" title="Livery Templates">
Always download the official, up-to-date vehicle templates from the developer community before applying your dark tactical textures. This prevents alignment errors on custom SUV and cruiser models.
</Callout>
