<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Undercover Operation Guide">

Executing a successful sting as an **emergency response liberty county detective** involves more than just driving an unmarked car. You must follow a specific protocol to ensure you don't blow your cover before the arrest is made. This process involves observation, verification, and then rapid intervention.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Surveillance Phase">
    Position your vehicle in a high-traffic area or near a known crime hotspot like the Jewelry Store. Use your **Binoculars** to watch for suspicious players or vehicles without getting too close.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="MDT Verification">
    Once a suspect is identified, enter their license plate or name into the **MDT**. Check for active warrants, expired registration, or if the vehicle is reported stolen. This gives you a legal reason to initiate the stop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Tail">
    Follow the suspect at a distance. Do not tail them too closely or drive erratically. If they are speeding, wait for a safe straightaway where they cannot easily maneuver into narrow alleys before activating your lights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Intervention">
    Activate your visor lights and sirens. Use the "stop" command in chat. If the suspect flees, you have the advantage of a high-performance police engine in a car they didn't expect to be a threat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Roleplay Tip">
When you pull someone over, don't immediately jump out with a gun. Approach the window and use the detective's unique dialogue options to ask for identification, maintaining the "plainclothes" persona.
</Callout>
