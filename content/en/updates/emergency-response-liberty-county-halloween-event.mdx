<Section icon={Target} title="Trick-or-Treating and Candy Farming">

Trick-or-treating is the primary gameplay loop for acquiring event currency during the **emergency response liberty county halloween event**. Upon spawning into the game as a civilian, a trick-or-treat bucket is automatically added to your inventory. Players must equip this bucket and approach residential houses spread across the neighborhoods to collect candy.

### Doorbell Outcomes
When you approach a house and ring the doorbell, one of four randomized outcomes will trigger. Three of these outcomes award valuable candy currencies, while the fourth triggers a minor setback.

| Outcome | Reward Type | Rarity / Impact |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Red Candy** | High-Value Currency | Rare drop rate |
| **Orange Candy** | Medium-Value Currency | Uncommon drop rate |
| **Yellow Candy** | Standard Currency | Common drop rate |
| **Trick** | No Reward | Triggers a jumpscare or minor hazard |
