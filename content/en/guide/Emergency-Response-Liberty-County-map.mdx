<Section icon={Trophy} title="Mastering Map Navigation in 2026">

Effective navigation on the **Emergency Response Liberty County map** requires more than just looking at the mini-map. Professional players use a combination of landmarks, street names, and the MDT system to stay ahead of the competition.

**Key Navigation Techniques:**
- **MDT Waypoints**: Use the Mobile Data Terminal to set GPS markers for active calls. This is the fastest way to find an incident.
- **Landmark Recognition**: Memorize the tallest buildings in River City and the unique silos in Springfield to orient yourself without opening the full map.
- **Traffic Patterns**: During peak server hours, main intersections in River City can become congested. Use the side alleys and parking lot pass-throughs to bypass traffic.
- **Fuel Management**: Always monitor your fuel gauge. The map features several gas stations; running out of fuel during a pursuit is a guaranteed way to get arrested or lose a suspect.
