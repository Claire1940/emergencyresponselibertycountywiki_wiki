### Core Fishing Mechanics
To start, equip your Fishing Rod from your inventory, stand near any body of water, and left-click to cast your line. You must wait exactly 10 seconds before a fish bites. Once a bite occurs, the minigame interface will appear on the right side of your screen.

| Mechanic | Action Required | Failure Condition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Casting** | Left-click near water | None (10-second wait timer starts) |
| **Reeling** | Click repeatedly to keep line in green box | Box turns red; fish escapes if out too long |
| **Rarity Check** | Observe green box movement speed | Faster movement indicates higher rarity |

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Fishing Tutorial">

Landing a fish requires steady hand-eye coordination. Follow these steps to master the minigame and secure your catch every single time.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Cast Your Line">
    Equip your Fishing Rod from your hotbar. Walk up to the edge of the water and left-click. Your character will cast the line, and a 10-second countdown begins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Bite">
    After 10 seconds, a visual indicator and a vertical bar will appear on the right side of your screen. This signals that a fish is officially on your hook.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance the Indicator">
    Left-click repeatedly to keep the white line inside the moving green box. If the white line drifts outside of the green box for too long, the fish will snap the line and escape.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim and Sell">
    Once the progress bar fills, the fish is added to your inventory. Drive over to Bayou Bill at the Fish Shack to sell your inventory for direct cash.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Warning">
Fish items are session-bound. They **do not save** in your inventory if you disconnect, crash, or leave the server. Always sell your catch to Bayou Bill before logging off.
</Callout>

</Section>
