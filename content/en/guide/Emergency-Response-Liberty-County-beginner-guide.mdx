<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step First Session Walkthrough">

Navigating your first hour in the game can feel overwhelming. Follow this structured progression path to establish a steady income stream, purchase a reliable vehicle, and complete your very first emergency shift.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Active Promo Codes">
    Before doing anything else, open your in-game Settings menu, select Promo Codes, and enter **ERLC2026** to receive an instant $10,000 cash reward. This starting capital is crucial for buying your first set of wheels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose the Civilian Team">
    Select the Civilian team from the team selection menu. This team has no entry requirements and allows you to explore the map freely without the pressure of responding to urgent dispatch calls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Take a Steady Civilian Job">
    Head to a marked job location on your map. Simple roles like farming or hospital work provide consistent paychecks. Work these jobs for a few cycles to build up your bank account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Buy a Reliable Starter Vehicle">
    Drive or walk to the nearest vehicle dealership. Avoid the temptation to blow all your cash on cosmetics. Purchase a balanced, affordable sedan or SUV that features predictable handling and decent top speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Join Your First Emergency Department">
    Once you know the roads, open the team menu and join Police, Sheriff, Fire & Rescue, or DOT. Head to their respective headquarters, equip your uniform, select your rank-appropriate vehicle, and prepare for dispatch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Game Debt">
Do not spend all your cash on vehicle modifications or expensive weapons during your first session. Keep at least $2,000 in reserve to cover fuel costs and minor equipment purchases.
</Callout>

Progressing in Liberty County requires a balance of cash (used for purchasing civilian vehicles and custom upgrades) and department XP (used to unlock higher ranks, specialized vehicles, and advanced gear).
