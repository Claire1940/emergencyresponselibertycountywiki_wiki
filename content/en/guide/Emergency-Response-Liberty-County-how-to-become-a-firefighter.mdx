Entering the emergency services in River City is straightforward, but mastering the role requires dedication. Unlike real-world recruitment processes, there are no complex exams or waiting lists. Any player can join the River City Fire and Rescue team directly from the main lobby or mid-game interface. 

Once you join the team, you start as a Volunteer. You must report to the main Fire Station to equip your gear and spawn your first response vehicle. This team focuses entirely on cooperative rescue gameplay, making it an excellent choice for players who prefer team-based activities over high-speed police pursuits.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Firefighter Career Path">

To help you transition from a novice volunteer to a highly respected Battalion Chief, follow this structured career path. Consistently completing calls is the fastest way to progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Fire and Rescue Team">
    Open the team selection menu from the main interface. Select **Fire and Rescue** to instantly switch roles. Your character will spawn at or near the main station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Your Uniform and Loadout">
    Locate the locker room inside the Fire Station. Interact with the wardrobe to equip your protective firefighter uniform. Open the equipment locker to grab your essential tools, including the Fire Hose, Extinguisher, and Medical Kit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spawn Your Initial Rescue Vehicle">
    Walk out to the vehicle bay or use the vehicle spawner menu. Select an available apparatus unlocked for your current rank. Volunteers start with access to utility response vehicles and basic engines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor the MDT and Accept Calls">
    Open your Mobile Data Terminal (MDT) to view active incidents. Look for structure fires, vehicle fires, or medical calls. Accept a call near your location to mark a GPS route on your mini-map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Suppress the Fire and Rescue Civilians">
    Drive safely to the scene with your emergency lights and sirens activated. Connect your hose to a nearby hydrant or use your portable extinguisher. Extinguish all active flame nodes and treat any injured civilians using your medical gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Fires in Liberty County spread dynamically. Standing too close to active flames without your SCBA gear equipped will rapidly drain your health bar, resulting in a forced respawn.
</Callout>

</Section>
